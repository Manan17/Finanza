About Us

The most anticipated of its kind, Finanza is Mumbai’s fastest growing annual, inter-collegiate Financial and Entrepreneurial Festival hosted by SVKM’s Mithibai College. Over the years, it has built a community of over 100 participating colleges, established country-wide connections with more than 20,000 finance and business enthusiasts and inspired a body of 150+ talented and driven students to form the foundation on which the fest flourishes. Finanza organises a spectrum of events ranging from Financial and Entrepreneurial Summits, FinFare (carnival) and ‘Fables with Finanza’ (our very own Talk Show), to ‘Mock Stock’ (Finanza Markets) and Musical Evenings.

As it gracefully preserves the legacy of its former editions, Finanza promises to soar ceaselessly,beyond its horizons, into the nexus of ambition and accomplishment. With immense pride and gratitude, we unveil our latest edition, or what we fondly call ‘The Golden Edition of Finanza’ a culmination of dreams, efforts, hopes and aspirations - for students, of students and by students.

Events

Fables with Finanza (Talk Show)

‘Fables with Finanza’ is Finanza’s very own talk-show that digitally connects with a stellar line-up of prominent personalities from all over the world to enrich the audience with their knowledge and understanding and start finance-related conversations and discussions.

Financial and Entrepreneurial Summits

Year after year, Team Finanza extends its invitation to a host of illustrious panelists from a gamut of experienced industry professionals, path-breaking entrepreneurs and iconic celebrities that share valuable insights and inspiring stories.

Musical Evening

Scintillating, exhilarating and rejuvenating, Finanza’s musical evenings promise the most memorable experience with live performances by celebrated artists and the coolest songs to vibe to!

Social Cause

Our engagement with social causes is a small way of giving back to the society that gave us so much. Team Finanza strives to steer revolution in its own little way and create a fundamental difference in the assumption of its duty. With responsibility, kindness, conscientiousness and compassion, we pledge to create an impact, one cause at a time.

Mock Stock (Finanza Markets)

The Mock Stock is Finanza’s virtual simulation of the stock exchange amalgamating its major aspects to provide a real-world experience to all of its participants. one cause at a time.
